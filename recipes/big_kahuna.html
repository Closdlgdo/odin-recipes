Big Kahuna Burger from "Pulp Fiction"

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cheese
  • Onion
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Instructions:

  1. Divide the ground beef into 4 equal parts and shape each part into a patty. Season both sides of the patties with salt and black pepper.
  2. Heat a grill pan over medium-high heat.
  3. Cook the patties for about 5-6 minutes on each side or until they are fully cooked.
  4. Toast the hamburger buns.
  5. Build your burger by placing lettuce, tomato, cheese, onion, ketchup, and mustard on the bottom bun.
  6. Add the cooked patty on top of the condiments.
  7. Top with the other half of the bun.
  8. Serve and enjoy!

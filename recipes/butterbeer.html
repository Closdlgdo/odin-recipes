Butterbeer from "Harry Potter"

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon apple cider vinegar
  • 3/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 cups cream soda
  • Whipped cream for topping

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan over medium heat, combine brown sugar and water. Cook until the mixture begins to boil and the sugar dissolves.
  2. Add butter and stir until melted.
  3. Stir in salt, apple cider vinegar, heavy cream, and vanilla extract.
  4. Cook for about 2-3 minutes, or until the mixture is smooth.
  5. Divide the mixture among 4 cups.
  6. Add 1 cup of cream soda to each cup and stir to combine.
  7. Top with whipped cream and enjoy!

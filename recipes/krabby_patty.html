The Krabby Patty from "Spongebob Squarepants"

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cheese
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine ground beef, breadcrumbs, egg, Worcestershire sauce, salt, black pepper, onion powder, and garlic powder.
  2. Mix until well combined.
  3. Divide the mixture into 4 equal parts and shape each part into a patty.
  4. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat.
  5. Cook the patties for about 5-6 minutes on each side or until they are fully cooked.
  6. Toast the hamburger buns.
  7. Build your burger by placing lettuce, tomato, cheese, pickles, ketchup, and mustard on the bottom bun.
  8. Add the cooked patty on top of the condiments.
  9. Top with the other half of the bun.
  10. Serve and enjoy!

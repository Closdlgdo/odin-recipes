Bubba Gump Shrimp from "Forrest Gump"

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cornmeal
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, cornmeal, salt, black pepper, garlic powder, paprika, and cayenne pepper.
  2. Mix well.
  3. Add the shrimp to the bowl and toss to coat.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  5. Fry the shrimp for about 2-3 minutes on each side or until golden brown.
  6. Use a slotted spoon to remove the shrimp from the skillet and transfer to a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.
  7. Serve with your favorite dipping sauce and enjoy!

Ratatouille from "Ratatouille"

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium-sized eggplant, cut into 1/4-inch rounds
  • 1 medium-sized zucchini, cut into 1/4-inch rounds
  • 1 medium-sized yellow squash, cut into 1/4-inch rounds
  • 1 medium-sized yellow onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes, drained
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Arrange the eggplant, zucchini, and yellow squash rounds in a circular pattern in a 9-inch baking dish.
  3. In a skillet over medium heat, sauté the onion and garlic until tender.
  4. Add diced tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, and basil to the skillet. Season with salt and black pepper. Stir and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Pour the tomato mixture over the vegetables in the baking dish.
  6. Drizzle olive oil over the top of the dish.
  7. Cover the dish with foil.
  8. Bake for 40-45 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.
  9. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes.
  10. Serve warm.
